What is "Product Expiration Reminder"??

"Product Expiration Reminder" is a website created to remind you about the expiry dates of the products. This website will notify you about the expiry dates of the products, along with few additional details about the day left of the products can be consumed or used, and the text message is sent via email. This website uses Artificial Intelligence to detect a barcode and a product and to classify the product, especially fruit based on Computer Vision (CV).

  • Barcode Detection
  • Expiration Date Detection
  • Fruit Quality Classification
